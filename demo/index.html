HOURLY EMPLOYMENT AGREEMENT

This Employment Agreement (the "Agreement") is made and entered into as of __________________ (the "Effective Date"), by and between:

Employer: ________________________________________________ (the "Company")

Employee: ________________________________________________ (the "Employee")

1. Position and Employment Status

The Employee shall serve in the position of ______________________. This is an at-will employment relationship, meaning that either the Company or the Employee may terminate the employment at any time, with or without cause, and with or without notice.

2. Duties and Responsibilities

The Employee shall perform such duties as are customarily associated with the position and such other duties as may be assigned by the Company from time to time. The Employee agrees to devote their best efforts, attention, and skill to the performance of their duties.

3. Compensation

a. Hourly Rate: The Employee shall be paid an hourly rate of $________ per hour, less applicable taxes and withholdings.

b. Overtime: As a non-exempt hourly employee, the Employee is entitled to overtime pay in accordance with the Fair Labor Standards Act (FLSA). Hours worked in excess of 40 hours will be paid at 1.5x the regular rate.

c. Payment Schedule: Wages will be paid [Weekly/Bi-weekly/Semi-monthly].

4. Work Schedule

The Employee's anticipated work schedule is [Describe Schedule]. The Company reserves the right to modify the schedule based on business needs.

5. Insurance and Benefits

a. Statutory Insurance (Mandatory):

  • Workers' Compensation Insurance
  • Unemployment Insurance
  • Social Security and Medicare

b. Company Benefits (Optional): Subject to plan documents and Company policies.

6. Time Off

The Employee shall be entitled to [Number] hours/days of PTO per year. Sick leave per applicable laws.

7. Confidentiality

The Employee agrees not to disclose any of the Company's proprietary information, trade secrets, or client data to any third party during or after their employment.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, the parties have executed this Agreement as of the Effective Date.

Employer:

Signature

Employee:
